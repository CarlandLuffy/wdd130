General Questions

Do I need prior rafting experience?

No experience is necessary! We provide expert guides who will teach you everything you need to know before and during the trip.

What should I wear for the trip?

We recommend wearing quick-dry clothing, water shoes, and bringing a hat and sunglasses for sun protection.

Are meals provided during the trip?

Yes! We provide delicious meals, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Vegetarian and other dietary options are available upon request.

What happens if the weather is bad?

Rafting trips run rain or shine! Our guides are trained to handle a variety of conditions, and we provide gear to keep you comfortable.

Can I bring my phone or camera?

Yes, but we recommend using a waterproof case or dry bag to keep your electronics safe from water.

Is there a weight limit for rafting?

Our rafts have a weight limit of 275 lbs per person for safety reasons. Please contact us if you have specific concerns.

General Questions

Is this a good trip for kids? What are the age ranges you allow?

Absolutely! Our rafting trips are great for children of all ages. However, we recommend that children be at least **8 years old** to fully enjoy the experience. Our guides are experts at keeping kids entertained throughout the journey!

Is whitewater rafting safe? I don't even know how to swim! Can I still come?

Yes! While rafting does have inherent risks, all of our guides are **certified in Wilderness First Aid** and have years of experience. Non-swimmers are welcome! All guests are required to wear **Coast Guard-approved life jackets** for safety.

What is included in the trip?

We provide all necessary camping and rafting equipment, including **sleeping bags, tents, life jackets, waterproof duffle bags, and meals**. Soft drinks, coffee, and hot beverages are also included.

What should I bring?

Packing depends on the season, but we recommend bringing **quick-dry clothing, sun protection, water shoes, a hat, and a reusable water bottle**. For a detailed packing list, visit our Contact Page.

What is so great about whitewater rafting?

Whitewater rafting is an **unforgettable adventure**! Imagine waking up under a starry sky, paddling through thrilling rapids, exploring remote canyons, and enjoying delicious meals by the river. It's the perfect mix of adventure and relaxation.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least **6 months in advance**. If you have specific dates in mind, booking a year ahead is even better as trips fill up fast.
